How to get started

Find a Facility

Using the Young Golfers find a facility map you will be able to find a YG coach near you. Then just simply get in youch with them and arrange a time to meet.

Register with a coach

At the time of meeting your YG coach they will explain everything your need to know about your child starting golf and if your happy will just ask you to fill out a simply form.

Start using Young Golfers

Once registered your coach will sign up to the Young Golfer app, allowing you full access to all the features and benefits of the programme.

Please select a facility on the map for more information.
